dumpthis.sh

A static archive for Maroon. Do you have information about a missing person related to New Life Technologies? Submit your stories here.
Think “public dead drop” not “blog.” Fragments live in /dump, longer pieces in /stories, art in /art, and formal downloads in /docs. The goal is to expand the fictional universe of Mar00n and its characters.
Style guide coming soon.

Author

V. Tracer. Mar00n is a Cyberpunk / digital horror fictional universe. The site is intentionally minimal so it loads fast, looks like a leak, and breaks as little as possible.

Contribute

Pull requests welcome for in-universe fragments. Keep it short (≤500 words), no real names or copyright material. There is no point in stealing, when we can do it better. V is the current curator; forks aren’t canon.

Official

Domain of record: dumpthis.sh. Donations and purchases only through links I post here. Don't steal when we can share.
