The Resistance

Dossier — internal archive

Dossier · Last updated: 2086

Overview

The Cyberpunk Resistance is a decentralized network of cells dedicated to undermining corporate rule and exposing the abuses of NewLife Corporation. Members operate across the Sprawl and hidden bunkers like the Nexus.

Structure

Cells are autonomous but coordinate through secure channels. Key figures include Maya and Neo, who specialize in field operations and cyber-warfare respectively.

Notable Operations

  • Operation Ghostnet — infiltration of corporate data centers.
  • Op: Nightfall — sabotage of supply depots.
